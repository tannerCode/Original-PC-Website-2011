Rappelling & Caving/waitomo

Get your trip started right away with rappelling into a dramatic limestone gorge, followed by black water rafting, an underground journey amidst waterfalls and caverns.

Rafting/kaituna river

Plunge into whitewater rafting fun on the Kaituna River, which has the largest commercially rafted waterfall in the world! Afterwards, soak it all up in natural hot springs under the stars.

Tongariro/tongariro ntl park

Spend the day hiking through diverse volcanic topography, all in the shadow of Mt. Doom (Mt. Ngauruhoe) from ‘Lord of the Rings.’

Camp & Hiking/rangeitiki river

Camp riverside beneath the starry southern skies. Hike amongst the beauty of the Rangiteiki river and perhaps jump off a 40 ft. dam!

Wellington/wellington

New Zealand’s capital city, Wellington, offering a spectacular harbor backdrop. Explore Te Papa National Museum, vibrant nightlife.
